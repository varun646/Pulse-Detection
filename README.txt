Remote Pulse Detection Demo
Carnegie Mellon Cylab

******************************************************************************************

EXPECTED RUNTIME:
-10 second video input @ 30 FPS: ~75 seconds
-10 second video input @ 60 FPS: ~140 seconds

******************************************************************************************

OUTPUT FORMAT:
The code generates 6 plots from the inputted video and saves them to the provided directory. The plots contain the following:
forehead_fft.png: power spectral density plot (PSD) of forehead

left_background_fft.png: PSD plot generated by Fourier transform of background to the left of the head

right_background_fft.png: PSD plot generated of background to the right of the head

mean_background_fft.png: mean of the PSDs of the backgrounds to the left and right of the head

forehead_background_combined_fft.png: PSD generated by subtracting the mean background PSD from the forehead PSD. The pulse is obtained by finding the frequency that corresponds to the max PSD in this plot. This pulse can be found in the top left of the plot and is denoted by a vertical line at the corresponding frequency

heat_map.jpg: Image where each vertical 1-D strip of pixels corresponds to a 1-D vertical segment of the forehead
